%	ABSTRACT
%----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

\Abstract{In the first part we analyze the intermediate layers outputs of a deep
convolutional neural network. The data extracted will be used to perform
information retrieval measurements, comparing the performance at different
layers. In the second part we perform the same measurements on a similar
network, after training the network with sparse filtering. In the third part we
propose a way to reduce the number of connections in the supervised layers by
including the label into the training process.}
